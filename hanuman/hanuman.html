This game is based on Indian mythology 'Ramayan'. Lords Ram's wife Sita was adbucted by The lord of Lanka 'Ravan'! Hanuman being a close friend of Ram,began searching for Sita everywhere. After months of searching, Hanuman finds Sita in Lanka. Lanka was a big and beautiful city protected by fierce demons. Hanuman conveys Ramas message to Sita; And to teach Ravan a lesson,Hanuman kills Ravan's demons and destroys the buildings of lanka

Objective of the game is to kill all protector demons of Lanka while causing as much damage as possible to lankan buildings so that it will be easier for Army of Ram to crush Ravan!

This game is built using actionscript 3.0 and powered by starling game engine which makes use of the power of GPU. All physics calculations are done using nape physics framework. Nape is avery lightweight and fast physics engine for flash. The source code of the game is available at Github.

we are also planning to release an updated version for android and Ipad devices.






                          

Click and pull the stone held by tale of Hanuman to the desired direction. Once you release the mouse button, tail will act like a catapult and the stone speeds towards the enemy base. Try to hit all the enemies with as less number of stones as possible.More damage caused to the buildings,will earn more points. Once all demons are destroyed, level is completed.
